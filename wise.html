About the Program

WISE is India's first exclusive training program designed to increase women representation in political consulting. This 100 Days virtual program offers hands-on experience, structured mentorship, and real-world assignments, equipping college students and fresh graduates with the skills and exposure needed to thrive in political consulting. More than just a program, WISE is a bold step towards making political consulting a legitimate and accessible career choice for women.

Eligibility

  • Indian citizens below 25 years of age
  • Women pursuing graduation or recent graduates
  • Can commit a minimum of 4 hours per day for 6 days a week
  • Should have hunger and intent to excel

Why Choose WISE?

  • Break barriers in a field still distant for many women and claim your space in strategy and leadership
  • Gain hands-on exposure across Political Strategy, Research, Campaigns, Communications, and Data Analytics
  • Contribute meaningfully to live political projects and shape the future of Indian politics

What Will You Gain?

  • An introduction to the inner workings of a political consultancy
  • Hands-on training in Political Intelligence, Research, and Communications
  • Direct learning experience from India’s senior-most political consultants
  • A strong foundation for a future career in political consulting
